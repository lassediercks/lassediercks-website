I am a Designer with a focus on the technical Design execution process of web interfaces, this is my manifesto.

I am creating design for humans. Always will that human being be the focus of my design.
I give feedback to anyone I want and I will recieve feedback from anyone who wants.
I am one part of a bigger process. I depend on the ones who crafts concepts as well as on the ones who bring my design to life.
I will keep my knowldege up to date. As the web moves fast, so must I.
I am biased. To ensure diveristy and avoid blind spots I need different people and opinions to challenge and improve my design.
I am not pleasing. I respect design as a craftsmanship and I will fight for good design. That can cause friction.
I embrace accessibility, from screenreaders to different viewport widths.
I believe in the power of design systems.
Thanks for reading.

This was created because I want to make a point on what my motives and itentions are.
I expect this to be a framework for my thougts, a guidance for my work. That means on the day I created this, it was true. Today it may be outdated.

If you like this, I'd appreciate a share.
